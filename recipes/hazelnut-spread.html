Hazelnut Spread

Description

Hazelnut spread sweetened with dates to retain the health
benefits of the raw cacao and is high in antioxidants.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Hazelnuts
  • 6 Medjool Dates
  • 5 tbsp Raw Griund Cacao
  • 1/4 tsp Sea Salt
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Coconut Oil
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Vanilla (or Vanilla Extract)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 275F (140C)
  2. Fill cookie sheet with one layer of hazelnuts
  3. Bake for 15 minutes
  4. Wrap hazelnuts in towel and rest for 10 minutes
  5. Remove hazelnut skins
  6. Add hazelnuts to processor and process until buttery
  7. Submerge dates in boiled water for 10 minutes
  8. Strain and save broth
  9. Add hazelnut butter, dates, cacao, salt, coconut oil,
    vanilla, and 1/4 cup of broth to processor and process until smooth and creamy
  10. Use immediately or store in refrigerator
