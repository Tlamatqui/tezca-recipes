Xocolatamales

Description

Xocolatamles are, as they sound, chocolate tamales.

Ingredients [Xocolatl]

  • 1 Cup Chocolate Hazelnut Spread

Ingredients [Masa]

  • 3 Discs Mexican Chocolate
  • 1 Canela Mexicana
  • 1 Sm Piloncillo
  • 1 Cup Non-Hydrogenated Shortening
  • 1 Tsp Sea Salt
  • 1 Tbsp Aluminum-Free Baking Powder
  • 3 lb Masa (Shortening-Free and No Added Salt)
  • 3/4 Raw Local Honey
  • 3 Dozen Corn Husks
    *Cleaned and soaked for in warm water for 30 minutes

Preparation (Filling)

  • See Chocolate Hazelnut Spread recipe

Preparation (Masa)

  1. Fine-grate chocolate
  2. On low heat, bring cinnamon stick, piloncillo, and 1 cup water to a simmer until piloncillo dissolves
  3. Strain mixture and set aside
  4. Whip shortening for 5 minutes or until doubled in size
  5. Whip salt and baking powder into shortening
  6. Add masa and 1/2 cup cinnamon tea gradually
  7. When mixture thickens, hand knead for 20 minutes
  8. Knead until bead-sized samples of masa float in water
  9. Knead honey and chocolate into mixture

Preparation (Tamalli)

  1. Lay corn husk smooth side up
  2. Spread layer of masa to husk
  3. Add hazelnut spread to center of masa
  4. Fold Tamalli
  5. Place in covered steam pot, open end up, filled to proper water level
  6. Bring to a boil then reduce to medium-low heat for 45-60 minutes
  7. If still mushy when unwrapping, cover and let sit for 30 minutes
